Index Fund Distribution Model

Just like traditional index funds distribute dividends to shareholders, PumpFun Index Fund airdrops tokens to holders proportional to their stake. All developer fees collected are automatically distributed back to the community, creating a true passive income stream for long-term holders.

How It Works:

  2. Automatic Distribution: Fees are pooled and converted to tokens
  3. Holder Airdrops: Tokens are airdropped proportionally to all holders
