How It Works

The distribution process is simple and automated. You collect fees from your memecoin project, send them to our distribution wallet along with your contract address, and we handle everything else.

The Process

  • Fee Collection: Accumulate trading fees or revenue in your dev wallet
  • Transaction Submission: Send fees to our wallet with your CA in the memo
  • Wallet Verification: We verify the transaction comes from the registered dev wallet
  • Holder Snapshot: We capture current holder balances at the time of receipt
  • Proportional Calculation: Distribution amounts are calculated based on holding percentages
  • Automatic Distribution: All holders receive their proportional share automatically

Security

Security is our top priority. Bonus Bot implements multiple layers of protection to ensure only authorized distributions can occur.

Dev Wallet Verification

Security Measures

  • Wallet Whitelisting: Only registered dev wallets can trigger distributions
  • Transaction Verification: Every transaction is verified against our database
  • Automatic Rejection: Unauthorized transactions are rejected immediately
  • Audit Trail: All distributions are logged and can be verified on-chain
  • No Smart Contract Risk: We don't require custody of your tokens

Why Dev Wallet Only?

Restricting distributions to verified dev wallets ensures:

  • Only authorized team members can trigger distributions
  • Prevention of spam or malicious distribution attempts
  • Accountability and transparency in fee distribution
  • Protection against unauthorized access

FAQ

General Questions

Q: What happens if I send from the wrong wallet?

A: The transaction will be rejected and funds returned (minus gas fees) to the sender address.

Q: Can I distribute partial fees?

A: Yes, you can send any amount you wish to distribute. You're not required to send all accumulated fees at once.

Q: How often should I distribute fees?

A: This depends on your project's fee accumulation rate and community expectations. Weekly or monthly distributions are common.

Technical Questions

Q: Do holders need to do anything to receive distributions?

A: No, distributions are automatic. Holders simply need to hold your token in their wallet.

Q: Are there minimum holding requirements?

A: All holders receive proportional distributions, but very small amounts may not be sent if they're below the blockchain's dust threshold.

Q: How do you calculate proportional distribution?

A: We use the formula: (Holder's Balance / Total Supply) √ó Total Fees Sent. This ensures fair distribution based on holding percentage.

Q: Can I verify the distributions?

A: Yes, all distributions are on-chain and can be verified using blockchain explorers.
